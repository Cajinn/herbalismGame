### Vendored files (renamed for web-friendly paths)
Tilesets: grass, water, tilled_dirt, fences, doors, hills, wooden_house,
house_roof, house_walls. Objects: furniture, grass_biom, plants, paths, bridge,
chest, tools_materials. Characters: character (192×192, 4×4 grid of 48×48
frames), character_actions (96×576, 2×12 of 48×48 frames).

Tilesets are 16×16 px tiles. The character sheets use 48×48 px frames.
